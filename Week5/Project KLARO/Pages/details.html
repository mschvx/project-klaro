About Project KLARO

Developed by Allane Lee Castro

Project KLARO, short for Knowledge-Driven Linear-Programming Analysis for Resource Optimization, is an optimization tool designed to determine which environmental projects yield the most cost-efficient pollution reduction outcomes using the Simplex Method.

This tool aims to promote transparency and data-driven decision-making in Greenvale to meet its citywide pollutant reduction targets.
